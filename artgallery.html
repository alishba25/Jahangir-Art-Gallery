The Legacy

Founded by Sir Cowasji Jehangir.

Since its inception in 1952, the Jahangir Art Gallery has been closely associated with the development of contemporary Indian art. The gallery was designed by Durga Bajpai and is one of the early concrete structures in the city.

It is not just an exhibition space; it is an institution that has hosted the works of legendary masters like M.F. Husain, S.H. Raza, and Amrita Sher-Gil, while continually providing a platform for emerging talent from across the subcontinent.

"A sanctuary where the city's noise fades, and the canvas speaks."
